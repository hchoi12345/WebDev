Jean-Georges



About:

Jean-Georges Restaurants are making an effort to support non-GMO farms. We are advancing towards the clean food movement in sourcing non-GMO product wherever possible.



Though Jean-Georges Vongerichten is one of the world’s most famous chefs, his skills extend far beyond the kitchen. A savvy businessman and restaurateur, Jean-Georges is responsible for the operation and success of a constellation of three and four star restaurants worldwide. Jean-Georges is involved in every aspect of his restaurants – concept, menu, architectural design, staff selection and trainings – in an ongoing effort to create restaurants that are both timely and enduring.
